<objective>
Human review pass over `PRUNE-VERDICT.json` per D-15. The human reviewer (Donovan) reads every PRUNE and every UNCERTAIN verdict, flips any that are wrong, rewrites rationales as needed, and produces `PRUNE-FINAL-LIST.json` — the authoritative input to Plan 03's prune-execution commit.

Purpose: D-15 establishes asymmetric risk: false-positive prunes are user-facing and harder to reverse even with `git mv`; false-negative keeps are recoverable in v1.3 with no user impact. The audit subagent runs unattended over 41 candidates; the human is the gate. KEEP verdicts are skimmed but not individually audited (the asymmetric-risk direction means KEEPs are the safe default).

Output: `PRUNE-FINAL-LIST.json` containing the human-approved subset of skills to archive, plus a math check confirming the post-prune count lands in the 75-85 target band.

This plan is `autonomous: false` — the entire plan is a checkpoint:human-verify task. There is no automated subagent step.
</objective>
